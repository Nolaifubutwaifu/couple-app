Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2025

Overview

Twosome ("we", "us", "our") is a private couple communication app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: email address and password (encrypted)
  • Profile information: display name, avatar photo, timezone
  • Messages: text messages and photos you share with your partner
  • Moments: daily timeline entries including text, photos, and mood selections
  • Game data: game states and scores shared between you and your partner
  • Date history: records of completed date night activities
  • Usage data: streak counts, achievement progress, and app settings (stored locally on your device)

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core couple communication features
  • To sync your shared content between you and your partner
  • To send local notification reminders (with your permission)
  • To display your online status and typing indicators (configurable in settings)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, our infrastructure provider. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Row-level security policies ensure that only you and your partner can access your shared data. No other users or third parties can see your messages, photos, or personal information.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or provide your personal data to any third parties for advertising, analytics, or marketing purposes. Our infrastructure provider (Supabase) processes data solely to provide our service.

No Tracking or Analytics

Twosome does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not track your behavior across apps or websites.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data — including messages, photos, moments, and profile information — is permanently deleted from our servers.

Your Rights

  • Access: you can view all your data within the app
  • Correction: you can update your profile information at any time
  • Deletion: you can delete your account and all associated data from Settings
  • Portability: contact us to request a copy of your data
  • Consent withdrawal: you can disable notifications, online status, read receipts, and typing indicators in Settings

Children's Privacy

Twosome is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of Twosome after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your rights, contact us at: privacy@twosome.app
